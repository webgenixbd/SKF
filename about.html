About our comapny

SKF DESIGN is one of the leading readymade Sweater garments buying agent & exporter and trading based company in Dhaka, Bangladesh. Since 2014. We are exporting sweater items worldwide. We produced high quality labeled garments. We prepare our brief in collaboration with our customers, based on the market requirements of a given territory and the requirements of individual customers

We have the expertise needed to fill the requirements of our customer’s considerable insight and specialist knowledge is essential, to be in a position to advice customers to ensure they get top quality at the right price. Based on the stipulations of the customer, we work out a brief together with our suppliers to achieve the optimum solution.

If you have any requirement kindly feel free to contact us with your specification like fabric count, design, style, size, color and your target price. Please don’t worry about the quality and delivery period of the product. We believe that best quality and best service sales the products and that is motto of our business. We may send you photographs & samples of your desired products.
